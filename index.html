About Us

Stray cats and dogs often inhabit harsh urban environments. They can be found in bushes, backyards, or even beneath buildings. Life on the streets makes them vulnerable to various dangers, including hunger, disease, and even violence. Stray cats and dogs also contribute to an excess population of cats, which can then have a negative impact on the environment and the welfare of the cats themselves. Therefore, we are here to help them find shelter, food, and be treated with dignity.

Join us in our journey to care for these furry friends. Together, we can make a paw-sitive impact!
